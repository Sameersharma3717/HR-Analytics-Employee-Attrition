# 👥 HR Analytics Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end HR Analytics project built using **SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI**. The objective was to analyze employee attrition, understand workforce trends, and develop interactive dashboards to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall employee attrition and workforce demographics
- Identify departments and job roles with the highest attrition
- Evaluate the impact of overtime on employee turnover
- Compare average monthly income across departments
- Analyze business travel patterns and their relationship with attrition
- Build interactive dashboards for HR and business stakeholders

## 📂 Dataset

- IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- Records: 1,470 Employees

---

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- The organization has an overall **attrition rate of 16.12%**.
- Research & Development recorded the highest employee attrition.
- Employees working overtime experienced higher attrition than employees without overtime.
- Sales Executives, Research Scientists, and Laboratory Technicians represent the largest workforce segments.
- Sales department has the highest average monthly income.
- Frequent business travel is associated with higher employee attrition.
